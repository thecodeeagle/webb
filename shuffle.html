ShuffleNet


It utilize point-wise group convolutions and channel shuffling and has 3 different stages. Within a stage, the number of output channel remains same. As we proceed to successive stage, the spatiotemporal dimension is reduced by a factor of 2 and the number of channels are increased by a factor of 2. V1 denotes version 1 of ShuffleNet and 2.0X denotes the 2 times number of channels compared to original configuration.
